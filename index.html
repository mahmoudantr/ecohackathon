FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers! Everything you need to know about EcoHack 2025.

Who can participate?

EcoHack is open to university students specializing in Computer Science who are passionate about environmental sustainability.

Do I need a team?

You can hack solo, but we recommend teams of 3-4 people. Don't worry if you don't have one; we'll have team formation events at the start.

Is it free?

Yes! Admission is completely free. We provide a place to hack for the entire weekend.

What is the focus of EcoHack?

EcoHack is all about creating innovative solutions for environmental sustainability. You can build anything from apps to hardware that helps make the planet greener!

Do I need coding experience?

No prior experience is required! We welcome beginners and will have mentors and workshops to guide you through your project.
